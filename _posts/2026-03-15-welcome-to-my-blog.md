Welcome to my new blog! This is the first post, and I'm excited to share my journey with you.

## What to Expect

In this blog, I'll be writing about:

- **AI & Machine Learning**: Latest developments, tutorials, and experiments
- **Technology Trends**: Web development, cloud computing, and more
- **Project Updates**: Behind-the-scenes of my personal projects
- **Random Thoughts**: Ideas, insights, and occasional rants

## Why Jekyll?

I chose Jekyll for this blog because:

1. **Simple & Fast**: Static site generation means lightning-fast page loads
2. **GitHub Pages**: Free hosting with automatic deployment
3. **Markdown**: Writing in Markdown is clean and efficient
4. **Customizable**: Full control over design and functionality
